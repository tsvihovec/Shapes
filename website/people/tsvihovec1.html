There once was a dog who had many friends.

He would act as a sort of hype man for his friends as he watched them chase cats.

Then one day his friends asked if he wanted to chase cats with them.

The dog agreed and began to chase a cat.

He chased for so long that he eventually became lost and became afraid that he wouldn't find his home.

He searched for so long that he became hungry and found the nearest cafe to eat.

It turns out that he found a cat cafe and was overjoyed at the amount of cats to chase that he wasn't afraid anymore.

Eventually his friends found him and all was well.

The End.
